What is it?

Walk Til Dawn is the ultimate gut check! This event will test the "Never Quit" mentality in all of us. This event allow participants to experience the rigorous and physical demand that each Ranger student endures to earn the covenant Ranger Tab. Walk Til Dawn, is a buddy team event which will be conducted in three phases for a total of 125 miles: Phase 1 (Jungle); Phase 2 (Mountain); Phase 3 (Swamp). All phases will be held in the State of North Carolina. Walk Til Dawn will not only test your physical skills but also your mental skills as you will be challenged to make decisions under fatigue and lack of food
